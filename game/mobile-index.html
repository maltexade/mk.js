MK.JS

Mobile Fighting Game

HOW TO PLAY

🎮 PLAYER 1 CONTROLS (Left Side)

  • D-Pad: Move left/right, jump
  • Down: Squat (blocks high attacks)

⚔️ PLAYER 2 CONTROLS (Right Side)

  • High Punch: Strong punch attack
  • Low Punch: Quick punch attack
  • High Kick: Strong kick attack
  • Low Kick: Quick kick attack

💡 TIPS

  • Each attack has different damage and range
  • Squat to avoid high attacks
  • Reduce opponent's health to 0 to win
  • React quickly and dodge incoming attacks

🏆 COMBO SYSTEM

  • Quick Combos: Low Punch + High Punch
  • Powerful: Jump + Kick
  • Devastating: Jump + Punch in air

ABOUT

MK.JS Mobile is an HTML5 fighting game built with Canvas and JavaScript.

Original Game: Minko Gechev (@mgechev)

Mobile Version: Enhanced with touch controls and responsive design

Technologies: HTML5 Canvas, Vanilla JavaScript, CSS3
